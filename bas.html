BĀS PVD

Consulting and design.

BĀS PVD Instagram

BĀS PVD is a roaming cocktail program specializing in class and comfort. BĀS was looking for a way to visually describe, their multi-night food and drink pop-up event series that featured rotating local chefs. Using the concept of music festivals as inspiration, I created the tagline "A Place With No Name". I also designed flyers that drew from music festivals, and early 90's skate zines as inspiration. Flyer designs highlighted the entire series as well as individual nights in the series. BĀS used the flyer designs and tagline as promotion for the entire series resulting in BĀS PVD taking over The Dean Hotel's dining space.
