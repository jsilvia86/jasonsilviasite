MXR Cocktails

Event flyer design.

MXR Website

MXR is a box-order cocktail kit that allows a person to become their very own bartender. MXR requested a flyer design for their cocktail class + tasting event, featuring RI based Rhodium Spirits. MXR's only requirements were to highlight the featured spirit Rhodium's citron vodka, as well as to use MXR's tagline "all you need is your spirit". Using MXR and Rhodium's own product photos, I designed a flyer inspired by food ads from 50's magazines. Flyer design was used by MXR to market their event on Instagram, Twitter, and Eventbrite. The event was sold out.
