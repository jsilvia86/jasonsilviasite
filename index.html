About

A multi-disciplined creative, from the creative capital.

Hello there. I'm Jason.

My background in both tech and media, have given me a unique perspective as a creative professional. I believe information and entertainment are not opposites, but are rather an essential pairing for every project I am involved in, regardless of industry. At the end of the day I'm a storyteller, so whether its through words, audio, or visuals, let me help tell your story.
