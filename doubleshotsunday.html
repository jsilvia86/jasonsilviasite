Double Shot Sunday

Name & logo creation, website and cover-art design.

Double Shot Sunday Website

Double Shot Sunday is a bi-weekly live streaming DJ mixshow combining the worlds of coffee and music. Along with streaming, Double Shot Sunday has also hosted events within various cafes featuring live DJs. The creator of the show requested a name and logo to reflect the main themes of the show, coffee + music. Using the 1970's Italia disco, double shots of espresso, and a DJ performing on two turntables, I created a logo that has a retro futuristic feel and utilizes the colors of an espresso shot. This logo has been modified and utilized for the streaming mixshow, flyers, cover art, business cards, and stickers.
