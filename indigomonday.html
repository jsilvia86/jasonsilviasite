Indigo Monday

Logo, website, and print media design.

Indigo Monday Website

Indigo Monday is a Providence RI based musical artist and DJ. The artist requested a logo + color scheme that reflected his name, would work on various forms of media + merchandise, along with being subtle enough to not confine him to a specific genre. Using the artist's love of indigo denim, coffee, and 1970's soul music, I created a logo that is simple, clean, and evokes a sense of nostalgia as well as feeling modern. This logo has been modified and utilized for the artist's website, flyers, cover art, business cards, and stickers.
