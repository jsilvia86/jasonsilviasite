The Creative Capital Show

Logo design and copywriting.

The Creative Capital Show Website

The Creative Capital Show is a Providence RI based podcast that tells the stories of creative entrepreneurs, with an equal focus on both the creative and business related aspects of an entrepreneur's journey. The host of the show requested a logo that would evoke a sense of professionalism as they wanted to highlight the business related content of their podcast. In addition the host requested a memorable tagline that could be used as a sign-off to end each episode. Using The New York Times as inspiration I created a logo that would convey the show as an institution for both the creative and business minded listener. In addition the tagline "Keep On Creating" was created as a simple and effective sign off for the show as well as being a call to action for the listener as the show aims to inspire creative entrepreneurs. The logo has been modified and utilized for the show's website and social media posts while the tagline is now used at the end of every episode.
